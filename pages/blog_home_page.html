ABOUT UDACITY

Udacity is a platfrom for any one interested in acquiring tech related skills such as

  • Artificial Intelligence: from Udacity school of artificial inteligence
  • Autonomous systems: from Udacity school of Autonomous systems.
  • Business: from Udacity school of business
  • Cloud Computing : from Udacity school of cloud computing
  • Cyber Security: from Udacity school of cyber security
  • Data Science: from Udacity school of data science .
  • Programming: from Udacity school of programming
  • Product Management:from Udacity school of product management

Udacity was founded by the two stanford instructors , Sebastain Thrun and David Stavens, for the initial purpose of introducing artificial intelligence to anyone who was interested for free with online courses. This initial endeavour saw the enrollment of more than 160,000 students from 190 countries accross the globe. ever since Udacity has been Training students in the field of tech and tech related fields .

With the aim to train the world workforce in the careers of the future, Udacity has resolved to a couple of efficient scheme such as

  • Creating an efficient learning platform .
  • Partnering with various enterprises, to provide training of workers / staff .
  • Partnering with the government , be it nations or federal.
