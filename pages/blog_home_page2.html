UDACITY, A TRUSTED PLATFORM BY THE GOVERMENT

There's alot of online learning platforms out there, but not all are truested by government, be it for training of civil servants/government worker, or scholarship grants for citizens by the government. Udacity is nut just any online learning platform , but one trussed by , not just individuals and enterprises but government of different countries e.g The United States , Egypt and Nigerian government. Due to a reliable servive provided by Udacity gorvernment are able to provide scholarships on various courses available on Udacity to citizens or gorvernment staff / workers . With partnerships like this gorvernment can not only provide free skill acquisition to youth but provide multiple skills for the youth which can reduce the rate of unemployment.

As a main focus , the Edo State gorvenment , with the supervision of the state governor Godwin Nogheghase Obaseki in collaboration with Udacity has provided scholarship for 1000 youths in Edo state to acquire the skill of front end engineering , data science and product management . such action by the government is found to be very efficient as alot of graduate are yet to be employed due to lack of companies to seek employement . With the skill set of Front-End Engineering, data science and product management one can work remotely. This will therfore create a bridge to the limitation of lack companies to seek employementas thes youths can work for companies in other states , countries or continent from the comfort of their homes .
